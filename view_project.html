RIP Lee's Buffet

Our goal is to welcome a new Asian cuisine buffet in the city of Troy, AL. The new restaurant is a startup franchise that needs help building their second location in Lee's Buffet's place. They have a sufficient funds for everything except dismantling the building, so they would be grateful to the help of City of Troy workers and volunteers. First priority will be given to high school students towards their college admission applications. Must be 18 years old or older to operate machinery. All city of Troy workers will be paid according to their normal rates. All volunteers will have their hours recorded plus given a giftcard to the restaurant worth $50.
